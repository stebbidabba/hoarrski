About Hoarrski

-BLACKWORK-DOTWORK-

Find me at @bleksmidjantattoo

Dm for booking and inquires

Hoarrski is a blackwork and dotwork tattoo artist based in Reykjavík. The focus is on striking contrast, clean execution, and thoughtful placement that moves with the body. Every piece is designed to heal beautifully and feel timeless years down the line.

Whether you have a refined idea or just a mood and a reference, I translate concepts into bold, readable designs. For bookings and inquiries, send a DM to @bleksmidjantattoo or use the booking form.
